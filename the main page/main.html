Verifies

VerifiesA CV, short for Curriculum Vitae, is a document that provides a detailed summary of an individual's education, work experience, skills, achievements, and other relevant information. Unlike a resume, which is typically shorter and tailored for specific job applications, a CV is more comprehensive and often used in academic, research, or international settings. It serves as a comprehensive record of an individual's professional and academic accomplishments, helping employers or institutions assess qualifications and suitability for positions or opportunities.
